Every Strike Unit has access to its assigned Watcher. If you're stuck in the field, you can request Intercepted Intelligence Support — but it comes at a cost.

Your Watcher will ask you to complete a small task in exchange for the hint. This keeps immersion alive even when a team genuinely needs help. Use it strategically, not lazily.

On CTFd, hints carry a point penalty. Plan accordingly.

01

02
Complete the exchange task. A fun challenge, rapid-fire question, or physical activity. No exchange, no intel.
03
Receive intercepted intelligence. The Watcher delivers one targeted hint for the locked node. Use it immediately.
04
